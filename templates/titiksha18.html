Titiksha'19

History

Titiksha has always been a grand festival of technological display, entertainment and joy in the SMVDU campus and every year since its inception in 2008 the students comes out with a new theme and then work on achieving a great technological success by organising various events. Every year various technological conferences are organised to make the students aware about the latest topics of technological advancement and of great concern. Various experts are called from across the country to deliver lectures and fill the young student's mind with apt knowledge. Research Papers are also displayed and acknowledged. Also every year students awaits the amazing star nights and other enjoyable performances by various eminent personalities like the Wadali brothers and stars like Ninja, Raftaar, Hardy Sandhu and many more. It is a night full of music, dance and loads of joy and entertainment and is enjoyed by one and all. Every year we strive to invite wonderful star performers to entertain our audience and this always goes by our expectation and proves to be a great success.
